Welcome to Karishma's Mehndi Designs

I'm Karishma, a passionate mehndi artist with over 2 years of experience. I specialize in creating intricate and beautiful mehndi designs for all occasions. Whether it's a wedding, festival, or any special event, I bring your mehndi dreams to life.

Explore my gallery to see my work, read my blog for tips and trends, or book an appointment to get your custom mehndi design.
